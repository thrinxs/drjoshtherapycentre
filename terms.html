Section 2

Nature of Services

Dr. Josh Therapy Centre provides professional coaching, therapy support, personal development guidance, and emotional wellness services. Our services are delivered by experienced practitioners and, where required, in collaboration with licensed clinical professionals.

Important: The services provided by Dr. Josh Therapy Centre are coaching and therapeutic support services. They are not a substitute for emergency medical care. If you are in immediate danger or experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact emergency services or a licensed psychiatric facility immediately.

Our services include but are not limited to:

  • Individual and couples therapy coaching sessions (online and in-person)
  • Relationship, marriage, family, intimacy, and mental health support
  • Personality development and emotional healing programmes
  • Digital book sales and distribution
  • Community outreach and NGO activities through NEHAL NGO
  • Internship and partnership programmes
Section 3

Booking & Payment

Booking process: Sessions may be booked through our website booking form, WhatsApp, or directly by phone. A booking is confirmed only after payment has been received and we have sent you a written confirmation.

Payment: All payments are processed securely via Paystack. By making a payment, you agree to Paystack's terms and conditions. Prices are listed in Nigerian Naira (₦) and are subject to change without prior notice, except for already-confirmed bookings.

Session confirmation: After payment, you will receive a booking receipt. Our team will contact you within 24 hours to confirm your session time, the assigned practitioner, and any preparation instructions.

Book purchases: Digital books are delivered to your provided email address after payment is confirmed. Once delivered, all sales are final unless there is a technical delivery failure.

We reserve the right to decline or cancel a booking where we determine that we are not the appropriate service for your needs. In such cases, a full refund will be provided and we will recommend appropriate alternative services where possible.
Section 4

Cancellation & Refunds

Client-initiated cancellations:

  • More than 48 hours before your session: Full refund issued within 5–7 business days.
  • Between 24 and 48 hours before your session: 50% refund or the option to reschedule at no charge.
  • Less than 24 hours before your session: No refund. Rescheduling may be considered at our discretion for genuine emergencies.
  • No-shows without notice: No refund. The full session fee is forfeited.

Centre-initiated cancellations: If we cancel or reschedule your session for any reason within our control, you are entitled to a full refund or a rescheduled session at no additional charge.

Multi-session plans: For Standard and Premium plans, refunds for unused sessions will be calculated on a pro-rata basis minus an administrative fee of ₦10,000.

Book purchases: Digital books are non-refundable once delivered to your email. If you did not receive your book due to a technical issue, contact us within 48 hours and we will resend it.

To request a cancellation or refund, contact us via email at support@drjoshtherapycentre.com or WhatsApp.
