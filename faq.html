🚀 Getting Started

No referral is needed. You can book directly online, via WhatsApp, or by phone. We have made it as simple as possible so nothing stands between you and the help you need.

Your first session is an initial assessment. We take time to understand your situation, what you are going through, and what your goals are. There is no pressure — it is a safe, unhurried conversation. Based on this, we match you with the practitioner or specialist who is best equipped to help you.

We typically confirm bookings within 24 hours. For urgent situations, reaching out directly via WhatsApp at +234 901 133 9978 is the fastest route to getting scheduled.

Absolutely. Many clients come to us without knowing exactly what they need — and that is perfectly fine. The initial assessment is specifically designed to figure that out together. You can also reach out to us beforehand and we will guide you in the right direction.

🗓️ Sessions & Format

Session length depends on the plan you choose. Online sessions (Basic Text and Standard Call) are 30 minutes each. In-person sessions range from 1 hour (Lite) to up to 3 hours (Basic) per sitting. Monthly and Premium plans involve structured sessions twice a week.

Yes. We offer both in-person sessions at our Port Harcourt centre and online sessions via WhatsApp. You can switch between formats as your schedule or circumstances require — just let us know in advance.

Yes. You can upgrade, switch, or add sessions at any time. We understand that your needs may evolve, and we are flexible enough to accommodate that without any complications.

Life happens. We ask that you give us at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule, so we can offer that time slot to another client in need. Please contact us via WhatsApp or phone to make changes to your booking.

💰 Pricing & Payment

Our pricing ranges from ₦5,000 for a 30-minute online text session to ₦3,000,000 for our comprehensive Premium plan (sessions until you are fully healed, up to one year). You can view all plans and details on our pricing section or the booking page.

Payment details are confirmed after you book your session. We will share our bank account details via WhatsApp so you can make a transfer before the session begins. If you have any questions about payment, contact us at support@drjoshtherapycentre.com.

The Premium plan (₦3,000,000) is our most comprehensive offering. It includes two sessions every week, ongoing support across all therapy types, priority access to our practitioners, three free books from our library, and sessions that continue until you are fully healed — with a maximum duration of one year.

We occasionally offer support for individuals in financial difficulty. If cost is a barrier to getting help, please reach out to us directly and we will do our best to find a suitable arrangement. You can also visit our Donate page — donations help fund subsidised sessions for those who need it most.

🔒 Confidentiality & Privacy

Absolutely. Everything discussed in your sessions remains strictly confidential. Your privacy is not just a policy — it is a core principle of how we work. You can speak freely without fear of judgment or disclosure.

No. We do not share your identity, attendance, or any details of your sessions with any third party without your explicit consent. Seeking therapy is a personal decision and we treat it that way.

Any personal information you provide — name, contact details, session notes — is handled with the utmost care and stored securely. We do not sell or share your data with third parties. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

🧠 Therapy Types

Yes. We work with individuals, couples, and families. Most of our therapy specialties — especially Relationship, Marriage, Intimacy, and Family Therapy — are designed to accommodate both joint and individual sessions.

We cover seven therapy specialties: Relationship Therapy, Marriage Therapy, Intimacy Therapy, Family Therapy, Mental Health Therapy, Emotional Healing Therapy, and Personality Development. Within each, we address a wide range of specific challenges — from anxiety and grief to infidelity, communication breakdown, and self-worth. See our full services page for details.

Dr. Josh Therapy Centre works in partnership with licensed clinical practitioners and medical professionals. If your situation requires specialist clinical care, we will connect you with the right professional — seamlessly and without interruption to your support.

Yes. Our Family Therapy services include support for raising teenagers and navigating parent-child conflict. If you are a parent seeking guidance, or if you have a teenager who needs support, please get in touch and we will advise the best approach.

💻 Online Sessions

Online sessions are conducted via WhatsApp — either as text-based sessions (Basic Text) or live voice/video calls (Standard Call). Once your booking is confirmed and payment is received, your practitioner will connect with you at the agreed time.

Yes. Online sessions can be just as impactful as in-person ones, especially when you are in a private, comfortable environment. Many clients actually find it easier to open up from the comfort of their own space. The key is showing up honestly and with intention.

Yes. Our online sessions are available to clients anywhere in Nigeria and the wider diaspora. As long as you have a stable internet connection and WhatsApp, we can work with you.

💙 Support & Follow-up

This varies depending on your situation and goals. Some clients experience meaningful progress in just a few sessions; others benefit from ongoing support over several months. We work at your pace and will always be transparent about what progress looks like for your specific case.

Clients on monthly and premium plans have a dedicated practitioner they can reach between sessions for check-ins. For all other plans, our team is reachable via email at support@drjoshtherapycentre.com or WhatsApp during business hours.

Your wellbeing and experience matter to us deeply. If a session does not meet your expectations, please let us know immediately. We will do everything we can to address your concerns — including reassigning you to a different practitioner if that would better serve you.

Yes! Dr. Josh has published four books covering heartbreak recovery, rebuilding trust after betrayal, integrity in relationships, and relationship foundations. You can browse and purchase them in our Library. Standard and Premium plan clients also receive free books as part of their package.
