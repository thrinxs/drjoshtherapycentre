Relationship Therapy

Relationships are where we experience some of our greatest joy — and some of our deepest pain. Whether you are struggling to communicate, healing from a breakup, or trying to move forward after being hurt, relationship therapy gives you the tools, clarity, and support to navigate it all.

👤 Individuals 👫 Couples 💻 Online 🏢 In-Person

Communication & Conflict

  • — Constant arguing with no resolution
  • — Inability to express feelings without fighting
  • — Silent treatment and emotional shutdown
  • — Misunderstandings and feeling unheard
  • — Different communication styles causing friction
  • — Saying hurtful things during arguments

Trust & Jealousy

  • — Jealousy without clear cause
  • — Deep insecurity in the relationship
  • — Snooping through a partner's phone
  • — Controlling behaviour and possessiveness
  • — Rebuilding trust after lies or deception

Emotional Distance

  • — Feeling disconnected from your partner
  • — Emotional unavailability in a relationship
  • — Growing apart over time
  • — Feeling lonely while in a relationship

Breakup & Heartbreak

  • — Inability to move on after a breakup
  • — Obsessing over an ex
  • — Depression and grief after a relationship ends
  • — Repeated breakup and makeup cycles

Situationships & Undefined Relationships

  • — Confusion about your relationship status
  • — Fear of asking for commitment
  • — Being strung along with no clear direction
  • — Catching feelings for someone who won't commit

Long Distance Relationships

  • — Communication breakdown across distance
  • — Loneliness and emotional isolation
  • — Trust issues amplified by distance
  • — Maintaining emotional connection

Unrequited Love

  • — Loving someone who does not love you back
  • — Obsessive attachment to someone unavailable
  • — Inability to let go and move forward

Moving On & Starting Over

  • — Fear of loving again after being hurt
  • — Constantly comparing new people to an ex
  • — Low confidence after heartbreak
  • — Readiness and preparation for a new relationship

Whether you are single, in a relationship, or healing from one — there is a session designed for exactly where you are right now.

Marriage Therapy

Marriage is one of the most significant commitments a person can make — and one of the hardest to sustain. Whether you are preparing for marriage, navigating conflict, or recovering from a devastating breach of trust, marriage therapy provides a structured, safe space to rebuild, resolve, and reconnect.

💑 Couples 💍 Spouses 💻 Online 🏢 In-Person

Pre-Marital Counselling

  • — Understanding compatibility before marriage
  • — Setting expectations and aligning values
  • — Financial alignment and money conversations
  • — Conflict resolution preparation
  • — Navigating family dynamics before the wedding
  • — Intimacy and physical readiness discussions

Marriage Conflicts & Unhappiness

  • — Chronic arguing with no resolution in sight
  • — Feeling unseen, unheard or unvalued
  • — Falling out of love over time
  • — Lack of effort or investment from one partner
  • — Deep incompatibility issues surfacing

Infidelity & Affairs

  • — Emotional affairs and inappropriate attachments
  • — Physical affairs and betrayal
  • — Rebuilding trust after cheating
  • — Deciding whether to stay or leave after infidelity
  • — Healing from the pain of betrayal

Sexless Marriage

  • — Complete loss of physical intimacy
  • — Mismatched desires between spouses
  • — Emotional disconnection affecting the sex life
  • — Medical or psychological factors involved

Separation & Divorce

  • — Processing the decision to separate
  • — Managing emotions during the divorce process
  • — Co-parenting arrangements and agreements
  • — Navigating legal and emotional complexity simultaneously

Recovering After Divorce

  • — Healing from the grief of a failed marriage
  • — Rebuilding identity and self-worth post-divorce
  • — Single parenting support and adjustment
  • — Readiness and preparation for new relationships

Blended Family Adjustments

  • — Step-parent and step-child relationship dynamics
  • — Loyalty conflicts and divided allegiances
  • — Establishing healthy new family structures
  • — Co-parenting with an ex while in a new marriage

Your marriage deserves a fighting chance.

Intimacy Therapy

Intimacy is one of the most misunderstood and least talked about dimensions of relationships. Problems in this area often go unspoken for years — silently damaging connection, confidence, and closeness. Intimacy therapy provides a safe, judgment-free space to address what most people are too embarrassed to say out loud.

👤 Individuals 👫 Couples 🔒 Strictly Confidential 💻 Online 🏢 In-Person

Low or Mismatched Sex Drive

  • — Stress-related or emotional loss of desire
  • — Hormonal or physical factors affecting drive
  • — One partner consistently wanting more than the other
  • — Rekindling desire in long-term relationships

Sexual Dysfunction

  • — Erectile dysfunction and related anxiety
  • — Vaginismus and painful intercourse
  • — Premature ejaculation
  • — Anorgasmia and difficulty reaching climax
  • — Performance anxiety affecting intimacy
🩺 Cases requiring medical evaluation are referred to our licensed clinical partners.

Fear of Vulnerability & Closeness

  • — Emotional walls preventing deep connection
  • — Fear of being truly known by a partner
  • — Avoidant attachment styles
  • — Difficulty being physically or emotionally close

Intimacy After Trauma

  • — Recovery from sexual trauma or abuse
  • — Rebuilding comfort and safety with physical closeness
  • — Processing boundaries and consent in a relationship
  • — Navigating intimacy with a partner post-trauma
🩺 Complex trauma cases are supported by our licensed clinical partners.

Rebuilding Physical & Emotional Connection

  • — Couples who have grown emotionally or physically distant
  • — Restoring affection, touch and tenderness
  • — Rebuilding emotional safety in the relationship
  • — Reconnecting after a period of disconnection
🌿
This is a safe, judgment-free space.

Intimacy challenges are more common than people admit. Speaking about it is the first — and bravest — step toward healing.

Family Therapy

Family dynamics shape who we become — and when those dynamics are broken, the pain follows us everywhere. Family therapy helps individuals and families understand their patterns, resolve longstanding conflicts, and build the kind of home environment where people can actually thrive.

👨‍👩‍👧 Families 👤 Individuals 💻 Online 🏢 In-Person

Parent & Child Conflict

  • — Persistent disobedience and defiance
  • — Communication breakdown between parent and child
  • — Generational conflicts and misunderstandings
  • — Authority struggles and power dynamics

Toxic Family Dynamics

  • — Manipulation and emotional abuse within the family
  • — Scapegoating — one person always blamed
  • — Favouritism and inequality between children
  • — Unhealthy power structures and control

Sibling Rivalry

  • — Competition and jealousy between siblings
  • — Long-standing conflicts that were never resolved
  • — Adult siblings still caught in childhood patterns

Estrangement & Reconciliation

  • — Cut-off relationships with family members
  • — Attempting to rebuild after years of silence
  • — Processing rejection from family
  • — Deciding whether reconciliation is safe or worth it

Parenting Support

  • — Overwhelmed or struggling parents
  • — Single parenting challenges and exhaustion
  • — Co-parenting conflicts with a former partner
  • — Disciplining difficult children effectively

Raising Teenagers

  • — Teenage rebellion and authority conflicts
  • — Academic pressure and performance anxiety
  • — Identity crises and peer pressure
  • — Social media influence and screen addiction
  • — Mental health concerns in adolescents

Caring for Aging Parents

  • — Caregiver burnout and emotional exhaustion
  • — Navigating the parent-child role reversal
  • — Family disagreements about elderly care decisions
👨‍👩‍👧
Families can be repaired.

No matter how long the conflict has lasted or how deep the wounds go — family therapy creates a path back to each other.

Mental Health Therapy

Mental health challenges do not always look dramatic — sometimes they look like someone who just cannot seem to feel okay, no matter what they try. Whether you are dealing with anxiety, depression, trauma, or something harder to name, our mental health therapy provides structured, compassionate support. Complex clinical cases are handled in partnership with our licensed practitioners.

👤 Individuals 🩺 Licensed Partners Available 💻 Online 🏢 In-Person

Anxiety & Panic Attacks

  • — Generalised anxiety and constant worry
  • — Social anxiety and fear of judgment
  • — Panic disorder and unexpected panic attacks
  • — Health anxiety and medical fear
  • — Fear-based thinking patterns

Depression & Persistent Sadness

  • — Persistent sadness and loss of joy
  • — Loss of motivation and inability to function
  • — Hopelessness and feeling stuck
  • — Prolonged sadness with no clear reason
🩺 Clinical depression requiring medication is managed with our licensed partners.

Trauma & PTSD

  • — Processing traumatic experiences and memories
  • — Flashbacks, nightmares and hypervigilance
  • — Emotional dysregulation rooted in past events
  • — Trauma affecting current relationships and behaviour
🩺 Severe PTSD is co-managed with our licensed clinical partners.

Grief & Bereavement

  • — Loss of a loved one and the grief process
  • — Pregnancy loss and associated grief
  • — Grief from divorce or major life changes
  • — Prolonged or complicated grief

Addiction & Recovery

  • — Alcohol addiction and dependency
  • — Drug use and substance dependency
  • — Behavioural addictions — gambling, pornography, social media
  • — Recovery support and relapse prevention
🩺 Medical detox and clinical addiction management involve our licensed partners.

Suicidal Thoughts & Crisis Support

  • — Passive suicidal ideation — not wanting to be here
  • — Active suicidal thoughts and crisis intervention
  • — Safety planning and immediate support
🩺 All crisis and suicidal cases are handled in direct collaboration with our licensed clinical partners. If you are in immediate crisis, please reach us on WhatsApp now.

Burnout & Exhaustion

  • — Work-related burnout and chronic stress
  • — Emotional exhaustion from caregiving or relationships
  • — Compassion fatigue in helping professions
  • — Loss of purpose, meaning and drive

OCD & Intrusive Thoughts

  • — Obsessive, repetitive thought patterns
  • — Compulsive behaviours that are hard to control
  • — Managing intrusive thoughts that cause distress
🩺 Clinical OCD requiring specialist intervention is co-managed with our licensed partners.
🧠
You do not have to fight your mind alone.

Whatever is happening in your head — we have the right support for it. No judgment. No pressure. Just help.

Emotional Healing Therapy

Some wounds are not physical — they live in how we feel about ourselves, how we react to others, and the pain we carry from experiences we never fully processed. Emotional healing therapy helps you confront, understand, and release what has been holding you back — so you can finally move forward.

👤 Individuals 💻 Online 🏢 In-Person

Anger Management

  • — Explosive, uncontrolled anger outbursts
  • — Passive aggression and suppressed rage
  • — Anger damaging relationships and career
  • — Learning to express anger in healthy ways

Loneliness & Isolation

  • — Chronic loneliness despite being around people
  • — Social withdrawal and avoidance
  • — Feeling disconnected from everyone around you
  • — Inability to form or maintain meaningful connections

Shame & Guilt

  • — Toxic shame that defines your sense of self
  • — Guilt over past actions that will not go away
  • — Self-condemnation and harsh inner critic
  • — Inability to forgive yourself for past mistakes

Emotional Numbness

  • — Feeling nothing — no joy, no sadness, no connection
  • — Emotional shutdown as a defence mechanism
  • — Disconnection from your own feelings and experiences

Unresolved Pain & Past Wounds

  • — Childhood wounds still affecting adult life
  • — Carrying old hurt into current relationships
  • — Unprocessed emotional pain from past experiences
  • — Patterns of pain that keep repeating

Rejection & Abandonment

  • — Deep fear of being abandoned or left
  • — Extreme sensitivity to rejection
  • — Self-sabotage rooted in fear of being hurt again
  • — Attachment wounds from early abandonment

Low Self-Worth & Self-Blame

  • — Feeling fundamentally unworthy or undeserving of love
  • — Consistently blaming yourself for everything
  • — Persistent negative self-talk and inner criticism
  • — Shrinking yourself to avoid taking up space
💙
Your feelings deserve to be heard.

Emotional healing is not a luxury — it is essential. Let us help you process what you have been carrying, and finally put it down.

Personality Development Therapy

Some people do not come to therapy because something broke — they come because they want to become more. Personality development therapy is for people who are ready to take intentional control of who they are, how they show up, and the kind of life and legacy they are building. This is where growth becomes a system, not an accident.

👤 Individuals 🚀 Growth-Focused 💻 Online 🏢 In-Person

Self-Esteem & Confidence

  • — Building genuine, lasting confidence
  • — Overcoming deep-rooted self-doubt
  • — Imposter syndrome and feeling like a fraud
  • — Learning to value and respect yourself

Identity & Purpose

  • — Not knowing who you truly are
  • — Lack of direction and life purpose
  • — Existential questions and meaning-making
  • — Finding meaning after loss, failure or a major life change

People-Pleasing & Boundaries

  • — Chronic inability to say no to people
  • — Putting everyone else first at your own expense
  • — Setting healthy limits and enforcing them
  • — Fear of disappointing others driving your decisions

Procrastination & Self-Sabotage

  • — Patterns of self-defeat that keep repeating
  • — Fear of success keeping you from starting
  • — Overthinking that leads to paralysis and inaction
  • — Avoidance behaviours disguised as productivity

Emotional Maturity

  • — Learning to regulate emotions under pressure
  • — Responding thoughtfully rather than reacting impulsively
  • — Developing empathy and emotional intelligence
  • — Taking ownership of your behaviour and its impact

Fear of Commitment

  • — Avoidant patterns that prevent lasting relationships
  • — Fear of being trapped or losing freedom
  • — Inability to sustain long-term connections
  • — Understanding the root of the avoidance

Life Transitions & Change

  • — Career changes and professional reinvention
  • — Relocation and adjusting to a new environment
  • — Major life events disrupting your sense of self
  • — Moving into a new season of life with intention
🌱
Growth is not accidental. It is a decision.

Personality development therapy is for people who are done leaving their growth to chance — and are ready to build it intentionally.
