The Founder

Meet Josh Gold —
The Antidote

♟ Growth Architect ⚙️ Systems Strategist 💊 The Antidote 🎓 Mentor ✍️ Author

Baridubari Joshua Joe-Amos — known across Nigeria and beyond as Josh Gold and affectionately called Dr. Josh — is the founder and lead practitioner of Dr. Josh Therapy Centre. The name Dr. Josh was never a title claimed. It was a name earned — given by people who experienced what happens when they sit across from him. They heal.

Josh calls himself The Antidote — because that is what he is to the people he works with. Where pain, confusion, and broken relationships have taken root, he brings clarity, structure, and a pathway forward. He does not just help people feel better. He helps them become better — at loving, at living, at showing up for themselves and the people who matter to them.

Since founding Dr. Josh Therapy Centre in 2020, Josh has served over 207 clients across relationship, marriage, family, mental health, emotional healing, and personality development. He brings to every session years of experience, a deep understanding of human behaviour, and an unapologetic commitment to results.

"People do not come to therapy because they are weak. They come because they are brave enough to want something better."

Beyond the therapy centre, Josh is a published author, a business consultant through Crescorp Grande, a tech founder through Thrinxs, and the architect behind Lucentia Institute and Averra Knowledge Academy — institutions committed to human capacity and growth. His forthcoming work, Smarter Than Einstein, is currently under academic review.

Our Story

How We Got Here

Every great institution begins with one person who refused to look away from a problem. This is ours.

2020

Dr. Josh Therapy Centre is Founded

What began as one man's commitment to helping people heal quietly grew into a full practice. Josh Gold founded Dr. Josh Therapy Centre in Port Harcourt with a single mission — to be the antidote to the pain, confusion, and broken relationships that too many people carry alone.

2020 – 2022

First 100 Clients Served

Through word of mouth, genuine results, and an unwavering commitment to confidentiality, the centre's reputation grew. The first 100 clients came — individuals, couples, and families carrying everything from heartbreak to deep mental health challenges. Every single one received the same level of care: total and undivided.

2022 – 2023

Books, Brands & Expanding Impact

Josh Gold published his first books — practical, honest guides to healing from heartbreak, rebuilding trust, and understanding what healthy love looks like. The centre also began developing its ecosystem of sister brands: Love Court, Peacemaker Call Centre, NEHAL NGO, Love O'Clock Podcast, SoulMate Search, and Lucentia Institute.

2023 – 2024

Licensed Partnerships & Specialist Care

Recognising that some clients require specialist clinical support, Dr. Josh Therapy Centre formalized partnerships with licensed clinical practitioners and medical professionals. This meant no client would ever be turned away — whatever their situation, we had the right person for it.

2025 & Beyond

207+ Clients & Growing

With over 207 clients served, four published books, and seven specialist brands, Dr. Josh Therapy Centre continues to grow — deepening its roots in Port Harcourt while expanding its reach across Nigeria and the world through online sessions. The vision of becoming Africa's most trusted therapy centre is not a dream. It is already underway.

Our Network

Backed by Licensed Clinical Specialists

Dr. Josh Therapy Centre operates in active partnership with licensed clinical practitioners and medical professionals. When your situation calls for specialist intervention — clinical psychology, psychiatric support, or medical evaluation — we do not refer you away. We bring the right professional to you, within our own network, maintaining continuity of your care throughout.

You come to us as one client. We bring you the right team.
