Section 1

Introduction

Dr. Josh Therapy Centre ("we", "our", "the Centre") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and how we protect it when you use our website, book a session, purchase a book, or communicate with us.

By using our website or any of our services, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services or submit personal information to us.

This policy applies to all users of drjoshtherapycentre.com and all associated services operated by Dr. Josh Therapy Centre, including its sub-brands: NEHAL NGO, Love Court, Peacemaker Call Centre, Love O'Clock, SoulMate Search, and Lucentia Institute.

Our core commitment: We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes. Your data exists solely to help us serve you better.
Section 2

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, as well as limited technical information from your device. Specifically:

Information you provide:

  • Contact details — your name, email address, phone number, and location when you fill in any form on our website.
  • Booking information — therapy type, session plan, preferred date and time, and any message you choose to share about your situation.
  • Payment information — when you pay for a session or book, your payment is processed securely by Paystack. We do not store your card details on our servers.
  • Purchase information — when you buy a book, we collect your name, email, phone number, and location for delivery and receipt purposes.
  • Application information — if you apply for an internship or partnership, we collect your professional details, CV information, and any materials you share.
  • Donation information — donor name, email, amount, and cause selected when you make a donation.
  • Communications — messages you send us via our contact form, WhatsApp, or email.

Information collected automatically:

  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Referring URL (how you arrived at our site)
  • General geographic location (country/region level only)
Section 3

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide our services — processing bookings, confirming sessions, delivering books, and connecting you with the right practitioner.
  • To communicate with you — sending session confirmations, receipts, booking reminders, and responses to your enquiries.
  • To process payments — handling transactions securely via Paystack and providing receipts.
  • To improve our services — understanding how users interact with our website so we can make it better.
  • To manage internship and partnership applications — reviewing and responding to enquiries from prospective interns and partners.
  • To comply with legal obligations — maintaining records where required by Nigerian law.

We do not use your information for automated decision-making, profiling for advertising, or any purpose beyond what is outlined above.

Section 4

Third-Party Services

We use a small number of trusted third-party services to operate our website and deliver our services. Each handles data in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • Paystack — processes all payments on our platform. Your card information is handled entirely by Paystack and is never stored on our servers. See Paystack's Privacy Policy.
  • EmailJS — used to send automated email confirmations and book delivery emails. Only the information required for your email (name, email address, order details) is passed to EmailJS.
  • Firebase (Google) — powers our website hosting and analytics. Anonymised usage data is collected to help us improve the site. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • WhatsApp (Meta) — when you contact us or book via WhatsApp, your messages are subject to Meta's privacy policy. We use WhatsApp only for direct communication — not for advertising.
We do not sell your data to any of these providers for advertising or marketing purposes. They receive only the minimum information needed to perform their function.
Section 5

Data Storage & Security

Your personal information is stored on secure servers protected by industry-standard security measures. We take the following precautions:

  • Our website is served over HTTPS, encrypting all data in transit.
  • Payment data is never stored on our servers — it is handled entirely by Paystack's PCI-compliant infrastructure.
  • Access to client records is restricted to authorised personnel only.
  • We retain your information only as long as is necessary to provide our services or as required by law.

Despite our best efforts, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you believe your information has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

Section 6

Session Confidentiality

This section is important. Everything discussed in a therapy or coaching session with Dr. Josh Therapy Centre is strictly confidential.

Our confidentiality commitment: We do not share what you tell us in a session with any third party — including family members, employers, or other organisations — without your explicit written consent.

The only exceptions to confidentiality, in accordance with Nigerian law and professional ethics, are:

  • Where there is a credible risk of serious harm to you or another person
  • Where we are legally compelled by a court order
  • Where you have given explicit written consent to share specific information

In all other cases, your session content remains private. This applies to our licensed partner practitioners as well — all partners are bound by the same confidentiality standards.

Section 7

Cookies & Analytics

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality and understand how visitors use the site.

What we use cookies for:

  • Essential cookies — required for the website to function correctly (e.g. maintaining your session).
  • Analytics cookies — via Firebase Analytics, we collect anonymised data about pages visited, time on site, and device type. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

We do not use advertising cookies or track you across other websites. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features of our website may not function as intended if you do.

Section 8

Your Rights

In accordance with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) and applicable law, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — you may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to correction — you may request that we correct any inaccurate information we hold.
  • Right to deletion — you may request that we delete your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Right to object — you may object to certain uses of your data, including analytics.
  • Right to withdraw consent — where you have given consent to data processing, you may withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 11. We will respond within 30 days.

Section 9

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.

For clients under 18 receiving therapy or counselling, a parent or guardian must be involved in the booking process and provide consent. All information related to minors is handled with the highest level of care and confidentiality.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information without appropriate consent, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to remove that information.

Section 10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of our website or services after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
