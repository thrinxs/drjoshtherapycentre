Where It Matters.

Dr. Josh Therapy Centre is a growing ecosystem of therapy, education, technology, and community impact. We are looking for passionate, driven individuals who want to gain real experience, develop meaningful skills, and contribute to something that is genuinely changing lives across Nigeria.

🎓 All current positions are  unpaid internships  — gain experience, a certificate, and a reference letter.

🩺 Clinical & Practice
🧠
Intern Hybrid

Clinical Psychologist

Support our licensed clinical partners in managing advanced mental health cases. Observe sessions, assist with documentation, and develop clinical skills under direct supervision.

  • Currently studying or recently graduated in Clinical Psychology or a related field
  • Understanding of basic psychological assessment and therapy models
  • Strict confidentiality and professional conduct at all times
  • 3–6 month commitment
🤝
Intern Hybrid

Guidance Counsellor

Assist with initial client assessments, psychoeducation sessions, and group support activities. Ideal for counselling or social science students looking for supervised practice hours.

  • Studying Guidance Counselling, Social Work, or Psychology
  • Strong empathy and interpersonal communication skills
  • Ability to handle sensitive information with discretion
  • 3–6 month commitment
👥
Intern Hybrid

Social Worker

Support community outreach, client welfare assessment, and NEHAL NGO initiatives. Help connect vulnerable individuals and families to the right resources and support systems.

  • Studying or graduated in Social Work, Community Development, or related field
  • Passion for community impact and social justice
  • Strong organisation and follow-through skills
  • 3–6 month commitment
