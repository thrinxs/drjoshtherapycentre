Six Types of Partnership

Whether you are a licensed practitioner, a company, an organisation, or a content creator — there is a partnership model designed for you.

🩺

Clinical Practitioners

Licensed psychologists, psychiatrists, and clinical therapists who want to handle specialist or advanced cases referred from our centre. You bring the clinical credential — we bring the client flow.

Referral Partnership

Corporate Wellness
