Your Generosity
Heals Lives.

Behind every client who cannot afford therapy is a person who still deserves to heal. Your donation helps Dr. Josh Therapy Centre and NEHAL NGO reach those people — funding free sessions, community outreach, and mental health awareness across Nigeria.

Your Impact

What Your Donation Does

Every naira given goes toward making professional emotional support available to more Nigerians — regardless of income.

🎙️

₦5,000

Funds one free WhatsApp therapy session for an individual who cannot afford professional support.

🏘️

₦25,000

Sponsors a community mental health awareness event reaching up to 50 people in underserved areas.

💛

₦100,000

Covers a full month of free therapy support for a client in financial hardship — 8 sessions included.
